Cournot-Nash Equilibrium
December 2021   -   Find on Github
Game Theory
Optimal Transport
This project is an academic project carried out in December 2021, it is highly inspired by this article. The goal is to Compute a Cournot-Nash (Equivalent of a Nash equilibrium with a continuum of agents) with optimal transport method, under separability asumptions on the costfunction. We designed a game were tourists chose their holiday destination, with the following constraints :
  - a travel cost
  - a penalisation on non-attractiveness
  - a capacity that ceils the density of agents in a destination
  - a penalisation on high density of tourists.
