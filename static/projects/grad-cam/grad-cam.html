Where do a neural network looks at when predicting that a cat is a cat and a dog is a dog ? The answer is in this article. The main idea here is to inspect the value of the gradient flowing in the last convolution layer. With a heatmap on this data, we highlight zones of the pictures that allowed the neural network to do its prediction. This academic work was carried out in February 2021, for the "Deep Learning in practice" class in MVA Paris-Saclay Master.
